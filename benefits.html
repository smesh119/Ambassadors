Mindful Meetups - Techniques to manage your energy
Mindfulness is gaining popularity as a way to better manage energy, maintain a sense of well-being, and strengthen resilience.

What is mindfulness? A famous teacher of mindfulness meditation, Jon Kabat-Zinn, defines mindfulness as a “means of paying attention in a particular way; on purpose, in the present moment, and nonjudgmentally.” Mindfulness is the act of being fully present – consciously paying attention to how you feel and what’s going on in the world around you.

Give it a try! Twice a week, you can dial into Mindful Meetups (hosted live in Raleigh) and learn how to relax, improve focus, and reduce stress. Learn more in the Mindfulness group: https://mojo.redhat.com/groups/mindfulness

MDLIVE - Convenient care options
As summer travels conclude and a new school year begins, getting sick can throw plans and schedules into disarray. Red Hat’s MDLIVE telemedicine benefit can help with flexible and convenient options to access care at a significant cost savings over current plan co-pays. Cigna and Harvard Pilgrim participants and their enrolled dependents can use MDLIVE for 24/7/365 access to board-certified primary care doctors and pediatricians via online video (including a mobile app) or phone. More than 120 Red Hatters have already registered for this benefit. Enroll now so you’re ready when you need an appointment!

Participant testimonial: "Red Hat's new telemedicine benefit [MDLIVE] rocks. Set up an account online, had a video consult with a pediatrician and 20 minutes later drove to the 24 hr pharmacy. Minimal life disruption and no missed school or work for anyone."

Learn more about MDLIVE, including how to register, at: https://mojo.redhat.com/docs/DOC-1076779
